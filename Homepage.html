About: Game Industry Experience

Game development is a team sport, and that means that much of the time is taken up with meetings. While it can be great interacting with other team members, there's always a gnawing frustration about needing to get back to your workstation and get some "real work" done.

Game designing is a creative and imaginative process that allows individuals to bring their ideas to life in a fun and interactive way. The act of designing a game involves combining various elements such as storylines, characters, rules, and mechanics to create an immersive and engaging experience for players.

Game designers often enjoy the challenge of finding the right balance between difficulty and enjoyment, as well as the satisfaction of seeing their ideas come to fruition in the form of a playable game. They also appreciate the opportunity to express their creativity and artistic vision through the design of game worlds, characters, and other elements.
